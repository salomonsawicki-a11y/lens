Lens

An AI reading companion for the web. Highlight a sentence, get a plain-English explanation grounded in what you're actually reading.

Lens is a Chrome extension that adds a small popup over text you highlight on any webpage or PDF, explaining it in context using Claude. Works on news, blogs, documentation, Wikipedia, journal articles — anywhere there's text.
